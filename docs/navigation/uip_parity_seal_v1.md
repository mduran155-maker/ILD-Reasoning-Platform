## 17. Final v0.6 Seal Statement

The ILD Reasoning Platform v0.6 UIP Parity Upgrade is structurally sealed.

The platform remains an evidence-governed clinical reasoning platform.

It is not a diagnostic engine.  
It is not a treatment engine.  
It is not public-ready.  
It is not a patient-facing tool.  
It is not a replacement for clinicians, radiologists, pathologists, pulmonologists, rheumatologists, microbiologists, occupational medicine review, MDD, or urgent clinical assessment.

Its purpose remains to teach and enforce disciplined reasoning:

- read the pattern,
- define the boundary,
- preserve uncertainty,
- expose missing evidence,
- keep mimics visible,
- allow overlapping processes,
- compare over time,
- distinguish acute overlay from chronic progression,
- prompt MDD when appropriate,
- prompt urgent review when danger is possible,
- keep working diagnoses reviewable,
- and avoid premature disease naming.

The v0.6 seal preserves the platform’s central principle:

> “It teaches clinicians to read change before naming disease.”

For UIP reasoning, the sealed expansion is:

> “It teaches clinicians to read pattern, boundary, mimic, time, missing evidence, review need, uncertainty, and change before naming UIP.”
