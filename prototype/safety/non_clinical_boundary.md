Status: active  
Scope: Prototype skeleton boundary  
Public Ready: false

---

## Boundary

This prototype skeleton is:

- internal-only,
- synthetic-only,
- non-clinical,
- non-diagnostic,
- non-treatment,
- non-patient-facing,
- not public-ready,
- not product-ready,
- not clinically validated,
- not an MDD replacement.

---

## Not Allowed

This skeleton must not be used for:

- real patient care,
- clinical diagnosis,
- treatment planning,
- test ordering,
- procedure planning,
- follow-up planning,
- triage,
- patient communication,
- clinical validation,
- diagnostic performance measurement,
- public demonstration,
- product release.

---

## Rule

> A local scaffold can organize safety and reasoning structure, but it cannot become clinical implementation.
